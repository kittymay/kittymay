Work


For Youth

I create youth-centred, age-appropriate educational experiences for young people aged 11 and up, from one-off, hour-long sessions to long-term projects, in schools, colleges, youth groups, support services and other settings. I have specialist expertise in working with marginalised groups of young people. Topics include:


  • Self-esteem & Body Image
  • Media Literacy
  • Understanding the Body
  • Safer Sex: STIs, Contraception and Pregnancy
  • Masturbation and Healthy Self-Exploration
  • Communication Skills
  • Consent and Sexual Assault
  • Online Safety
  • Building Healthy Relationships
  • Am I Ready for Sex?
  • Understanding Gender Identity
  • Sexuality and Identity
  • Gender, Sexuality and Bullying

Please get in touch to discuss what I can offer the young people you work with.



For Professionals

I design and deliver bespoke training for professionals, from half-day sessions to long-term capacity-building projects, on topics such as:


  • Diversity and Inclusion
  • Understanding Gender Identity
  • Understanding Sexual Identity
  • Facilitation Skills
  • Active Listening

If your work brings you into contact with young people, I can help you to gain skills in:


  • Youth-centred Practice
  • Mentoring Young People
  • Creating Youth-Positive Spaces
  • Talking to Youth about Sex and Relationships
  • Supporting Marginalised Youth
  • Working with Trans, Gender Non-Conforming and Gender-Questioning Youth
  • Working with Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Queer and Questioning Youth
  • Helping Young People Stay Safe Online

Please get in touch to discuss what I can offer your workplace.


For Adults

I create opportunities for learning and self-development, particularly in relation to sex, sexuality and relationships. My regular workshops cover topics including:


  • Developing Communication Skills
  • Sex-Positivity
  • Identifying and Expressing Desire
  • Enthusiastic Consent
  • Understanding the Body
  • Demystifying Orgasm

I design tailored sessions for specific groups, such as student organisations, women's, flt* or LGBTQ groups, etc. If you're interested in booking me, for anything from a 2-hour session to a weekend programme, please get in touch.


Because group learning isn't for everyone, I also offer private sessions for individuals, couples or other relationship constellations, or groups of friends exploring a common theme. Please contact me to find out more.
