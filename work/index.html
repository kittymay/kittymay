for adults:

I create self-development and learning opportunities, particularly in relation to sex, sexuality and relationships.


I tailor sessions for specific groups, such as student organisations, women's, flt* or LGBTQ groups. Options range from 2-hour workshops to weekend programmes.


I also offer private sessions for individuals, couples or other relationship constellations, or groups of friends.

topics include:

  • Developing Communication Skills
  • Sex-Positivity
  • Identifying and Expressing Desire
  • Enthusiastic Consent
  • Understanding the Body
  • Demystifying Orgasm

for youth:

I create youth-centred, age-appropriate educational experiences for young people aged 11 and up, from hour-long, one-off sessions to ongoing projects in schools, colleges, youth groups, support services and other settings. I have specialist expertise in working with marginalised groups of young people.

topics include:

  • Self-esteem & Body Image
  • Building Healthy Relationships
  • Media Literacy
  • Safer Sex
  • Masturbation and Healthy Self-Exploration
  • Communication and Consent
  • Online Safety
  • Am I Ready for Sex?
  • Gender, Sexuality and Identity

for professionals:

I design and deliver bespoke training for professionals, from half-day sessions to long-term projects. I can offer specialised support for those working with young people.

topics include:

  • Diversity and Inclusion
  • Understanding Gender Identity
  • Understanding Sexual Identity
  • Facilitation Skills
  • Active Listening
  • Youth-centred Practice
  • Talking to Youth about Sex and Relationships
  • Working with Marginalised Youth
